# Ultim8Soft

Ultim8Soft builds free, browser-only developer utilities. Every tool runs entirely client-side — no signup, no tracking, no upload of your data to any server. Visit the tool hub at **[tools.ultim8soft.com](https://tools.ultim8soft.com)**.

## Tools

- [Base64 Encoder](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/base64-encoder.html) — Encode text and files to Base64 format.
- [Base64 Decoder](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/base64-decoder.html) — Decode Base64 back to its original text or binary form.
- [URL Encoder](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/url-encoder.html) — Percent-encode strings for safe transport in URLs.
- [URL Decoder](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/url-decoder.html) — Decode percent-encoded URL components back to text.
- [GZIP Compressor](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/gzip-compressor.html) — Compress text and files with GZIP in your browser.
- [GZIP Decompressor](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/gzip-decompressor.html) — Decompress GZIP-encoded payloads back to their original form.
- [JSON Formatter](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/json-formatter.html) — Format, validate, and minify JSON in your browser.
- [JWT Decoder](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/jwt-decoder.html) — Decode JSON Web Tokens and verify HS256/384/512 signatures.
- [Hash Generator](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/hash-generator.html) — Generate MD5, SHA-1, SHA-256, and SHA-512 digests in your browser.
- [QR Code Generator](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/qr-generator.html) — Generate watermark-free QR codes from text or URLs, fully in your browser.
- [UUID Generator](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/uuid-generator.html) — Generate RFC 4122 v4/v1 and RFC 9562 v7 UUIDs in your browser.
- [Unix Timestamp Converter](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/unix-timestamp.html) — Convert epoch timestamps to dates and back, across multiple timezones.
- [Password Generator](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/password-generator.html) — Generate strong passwords and passphrases in your browser.
- [Color Converter](https://tools.ultim8soft.com/color-converter.html) — Convert HEX, RGB, HSL, HWB, OKLCH, and check WCAG contrast.

## Why browser-only?

The whole catalog is designed around a simple promise: your inputs never leave your machine.

- **Client-side only** — every encode, decode, hash, and format runs in your browser via JavaScript and the Web Crypto / Compression Streams APIs. There is no backend to receive your data.
- **No signup, no accounts** — open a tool, use it, close the tab.
- **No tracking, no analytics on input** — we don't log what you paste, upload, or generate.
- **No uploads** — files you drag in are processed locally; nothing leaves the page.

That guarantee is verifiable: every page ships a static HTML/CSS/JS bundle and you can read the source in your browser's dev tools.
